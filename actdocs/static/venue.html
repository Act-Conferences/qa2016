Travel directions

Public transport

You can get public transportation infos here: Travel Information
Start: Berlin Hauptbahnhof (main station), Berlin Ostbahnhof or Flughafen Tegel (Airport)
Destination: U Moritzplatz

Tickets

The tariff zone you need is "AB". Usually you take a single ticket for 2.70€ which will be good for two hours.
You get it at a ticket vending machine found at the S-Bahn/subway platforms. Since these tickets are not time bound automatically, you have to stamp the ticket when starting your trip at one of the red stamping machines near the vending machines.
If you buy your ticket in the bus, there's no need to stamp it.

If you arrive by public transport and leave at station Moritzplatz:
The subway exits are around a roundabout traffic, and the signposts there can be a bit confusing.
The roundabout traffic is crossed by the streets "Oranienstrasse" and "Prinzenstrasse".
Having a look at the map should help you find your way to the hotel/venue.

Train

From Berlin main station:
  • Take the S-Bahn S5 (direction Hoppegarten or Strausberg Nord) or S75 (direction Ostbahnhof or Wartenberg)
  • Change at "Jannowitzbrücke" into subway U8 (direction Hermannstrasse)
  • Leave subway after two stops at "Moritzplatz"
If your train goes to Ostbahnhof instead of/additionally to main station, you can also start from there, since this is a little bit closer to the venue.


From Berlin Ostbahnhof:
  • Take one of the S-Bahn trains S5, S7, S75 direction Spandau, Potsdam or Olympiastadion.
  • Leave after only one stop at Jannowitzbrücke
  • Change at Jannowitzbrücke into subway U8 (direction Hermannstrasse)
  • Leave subway after two stops at "Moritzplatz"

From Tegel airport (TXL):

  • Take Bus 128 direction Osloer Str.
  • Change at Osloer Str. into subway U8 direction Hermannstr.
  • Leave subway at "Moritzplatz"
or:
  • Take Bus TXL direction Alexanderplatz
  • Change at "S Beusselstr." into S-Bahn S41 direction Ringbahn (circle line)
  • Change at "Gesundbrunnen" into subway U8 direction Hermannstr.
  • Leave subway at "Moritzplatz"

From Schönefeld airport (SXF):

Please note: Schönefeld is in tariff zone C, so you have to buy a "ABC" ticket for 3.30€. The distance to the S-Bahn station Schönefeld is about 500 meters from the airport.
  • Take S-Bahn S45 direction Birkenwerder
  • Change at "Hermannstr." into U8 direction Wittenau
  • Leave subway at "Moritzplatz"
or
  • Take S-Bahn S9 direction Bernau
  • Change at "Baumschulenweg" (same platform) into S-Bahn 47 direction Hermannstr.
  • Change at "Hermannstr." into U8 direction Wittenau
  • Leave subway at "Moritzplatz"
or
  • Take RB14 direction Nauen
  • Change at "Alexanderplatz" into subway U8 direction Hermannstr.
  • Leave subway at "Moritzplatz"
